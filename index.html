SYSYTEM REQUIREMENTS

Functional Requirements

There will be two main user types on our application. Those will be Canteen holder/manager and user/customer. First group will be separated as Canteen Holder, Canteen Manager and Canteen Worker and the customer side will be separated by the needs of the holder. Default solution will be teachers, officers and students/normal users. All will be adjustable to the current work flow.

Both customer types will be able to use mobile app and both customer types will be scored as their shopping attitudes. Those scores will let the users take advantage of some defined campaigns and give a chance them to have a word on some of our features such as song list determination.

Manager side will able to control stocks, current financial condition of the store and some advices will be helpful according to their throughput. Advices will be such a feature that always gets updated and redesign itself time after time.



Non-Functional Requirements

Performance

Our program will be lightweighted and well-designed app which will be powered by a strong server-side machine as it’s not decided but planned like at least 100MBit internet connection, 2 GB RAM and 2 Cored Processor.

Scalability

Our system would handle at least 1000-1500 users for first steps and if needed that will be easy solutions to upgrade it and transfer the data between the machines.

Security

Backing up the data and secure programming style will be followed throught. And sensitive datas such as the user identities and passwords will be hashed with SHA256 or SHA512. And user roles user roles will be seperated between users, admins and for the rest of the roles.




Maintainability

Our probable technologies will not be shortlived ones because Java Spring technology is still rising. Client-side will be both browser-web application and Android app. So no maintainability problem will bother us and the users for mid-term.

Usability

Easy-to-use UI will be supported with globally understandable icons and documentation will also be clear. For any kind of problem and misunderstood part of the program, our wiki page will be active.




Availability

Our domain name provider will be one of the most relied and accessible by all over the world one such as GoDaddy or Name.com. We will not have such an availability problem.

Reliability

Our programs will be served on various platforms and those should be supported by many machines. On Android platform, our probable support will be higher than version 4.4 KitKat and on web service , any modern browser will support our app and those all will be tested.
